What is StackX ?

StackX is an intiative where we develop and distribute various mobile applications covering vast genres. From Astrology application to Hypercasual games and much more, we aim to make simple yet powerful apps just for you.

We strongly believe in providing you with the best possible apps and therefore we focus to take your feedback on every step to customize the app for your ease of use and convinience.

In this endeavour we are not just alone, you are also a part of this journey and our destination is to make something much more, something extraordinary and winsome.

When we say we believe in Excellence beyond perfection, trust us, we mean it !
